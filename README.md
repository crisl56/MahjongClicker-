# 🀄 Mahjong Clicker 🀄

## About 
This is a Unity Incremental Game. The Final for VFS PG29 A3 Mobile T5.  
Its a clicker game wher you click the mahjong tile.  
It goes to 10^303 with suffixes and higher without any.

## How To play
Click on the mahjong tile in the middle. Purchase upgrades  
Mahjong.

## Notes
The Game saves and loads on its own.  
It also increase offline with a max of 24 hours.
